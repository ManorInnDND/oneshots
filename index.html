Adventure Chronicles

Below are details for the different one shots.

White Plume Mountain

BK, Brendan, Charlie, Joshua, Courtney

You are a group of mecenaries hired by a merchant group to recover three powerful weapons from a theiving wizard, name Keraptis. You have made it to his lair, a dungeon hidden below White Plume Mountain. Two of you have perished in the dungeon, but the other two have recovered two of the powerful weapons.

Keraptis Commandos

BK, Brendan, Charlie, Luke, Sarah

The evil wizard Keraptis has captured you and fitted you with a collar that will supposedly kill you in one day unless you manage to slay his rival, Belamor. Keraptis has a portal into Belamor's lair, once Belamor is destroyed your collar will fall off and you will be free once more.

Desert Dummies

BK, Brendan, Charlie

Anilmis of House Qacha (Brendan), Mutthow the Slaughter (BK), and Khleo of Lost Chants (Charlie) are a band of companions in the unsavory city of Revlor, located near the Anauroch Desert. The comapnions are a ranger with a 'critter' lizard companion, a stoner druid, and a ambitious and bitter servant of the mighty wizard Telvar. They meet with Drew, a ratling druid who told them where to find some water in need of cleansing before returning to his rat wife and disturbing Khleo. These companions cleansed a water source in the undercity and convinced Anilmis's druid leader, the beautiful dryad Orion, to magic some of the water up to a spot on the surface where the water will be spread to the townsfolk for good (really for profit by the companions). Khleo made such a show of concern for the townsfolk that he is now known as 'full-heart' by the druid circle. Back on the surface the companions went to a shop owned by Mulkat, Telvar's star pupil-turned-competitor, and bullied a kobold into betraying his clone/brother kobold's keystone that will activate a portal in a nearby shack and grant access to Murkat's ziggaraut. The party hopes to either recover Telvar's stolen 'Compendium of Cruel Castings' from Murkat or demand Murkat teach Khleo sorcery. They steal a keystone from Kobold 7, and then while scuffling with Kobold 2 outsite the shack they witness him explode as his skeleton elongates into an ogre-sized frame and tears critter to bits. They kill the skeleton, go into the shack, and prepare to enter the fireplace/portal.
